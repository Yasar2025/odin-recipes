Lasagna

With its long history dating back thousands of years, lasagna is a popular Italian meal made with layered pasta sheets and flavorful toppings. It originated in ancient Greek and Roman cuisines when a dish known as “lagoon” was prepared using layers of pasta dough.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles

  • A Cheese mixture

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400ºF
  2. Brown the beef and onion.
  3. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce.
  4. Begin layering the lasagna.
  5. Continue layering the lasagna.
  6. Bake the lasagna for 1 hour.
  7. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and finish baking.
