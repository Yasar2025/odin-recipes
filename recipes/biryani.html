Biryani

Biryani is a rice-based dish from the Indian subcontinent, typically featuring long-grain rice (like basmati) layered with spiced meat or vegetables. It's known for its aromatic blend of spices, herbs, and often, fragrant elements like saffron and rosewater. The rice is often cooked separately and then layered with the meat or vegetables, which are also seasoned and often marinated.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Ginger, Garlic paste
  • Pepper and Cumin
  • Ghee
  • Tomatoes, Onions and Carrots
  • Curd
  • Coriander leaves
  • Raisins
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Marinate meat with yogurt and spices.
  4. Layer rice, meat, and fried onions in a pot.
  5. Add saffron milk or water.
  6. Cook on low heat until rice is done.
  7. Garnish with coriander and mint.
  8. Serve hot.
